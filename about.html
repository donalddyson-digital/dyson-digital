My Story

Why I Started Dyson Digital

I started Dyson Digital because I kept seeing the same problem everywhere in Metro Detroit — great local businesses with bad websites, or no website at all. Business owners were losing customers before those customers ever walked in the door.

I work directly with business owners — no middlemen, no agency overhead, no inflated pricing. You get a professional, custom-built website at a price that actually makes sense for a small business.

Every site I build is hand-coded, mobile-friendly, and designed to help your business grow — not just to look good. I'm based in Royal Oak and available for in-person meetings on weekends.
