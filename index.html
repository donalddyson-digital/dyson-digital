Why this matters

Most freelancers ask for payment upfront and disappear. At Dyson Digital, you only pay 50% to start and 50% when you're happy with the result — your investment is always protected.

01

50% Deposit to Start

Once we agree on scope and price, a 50% deposit secures your spot and kicks off the project. I'll send you a Wave invoice by email — pay securely by card in minutes.

02

Preview & Revisions

You receive a preview link to review your site. One round of revisions is included. We make sure everything looks and works exactly how you want it.

03

50% Due at Launch

Once you've approved the final site, the remaining 50% is due before we go live. Your site is published and you own everything — domain, code, accounts.
